Reseller Program

TinyTeam works with selected partners who help families, communities, and organizations discover digital preservation. All libraries are still personally reviewed, assembled, and delivered by TinyTeam.

At a glance

  • Commission: 20% flat commission
  • Model: referral + concierge (not a template resale license)
  • Tracking: client submits your reseller code inside the Start form
  • Payout: manual payout after payment is confirmed
  • Quality: approval required — TinyTeam is human-handled and curated

How it works

  1. Apply using the form below.
  2. Get approved and receive your unique reseller code.
  3. Share TinyTeam through your own content, community, or funnel.
  4. Client submits their request and enters your code.
  5. Commission is recorded after payment is confirmed.

Can resellers use funnels?

Yes — resellers may create educational or promotional funnels. However, TinyTeam remains the system owner and the official intake point.

  • Funnels may guide clients to TinyTeam’s official Start Your Library page.
  • Payments and submissions must happen through TinyTeam.
  • Resellers may not promise reusable templates, editable source files, or “forever hosting”.

Partner rules (important)

To protect families and preserve quality, resellers must follow these rules:

  • No selling or sharing TinyTeam templates or internal files.
  • No changing prices, offering discounts, or promising timelines.
  • No claiming “exclusive software ownership” — TinyTeam delivers finished archives.
  • No “eternal cloud storage” promises — the client receives a portable, client-owned archive.
  • TinyTeam may revoke reseller access if the program is misrepresented.
