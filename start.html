Start Your Library

TinyTeam builds calm, privacy-first digital libraries for families and communities — carefully assembled by humans, one project at a time.

Your library is designed to become an heirloom, with optional print-ready formatting if you prefer a physical hard copy.

Before you submit

  • Do not upload detailed family or community data on this page.
  • This form is for library request, package selection, and payment proof only.
  • All projects begin after manual review and validation.

After validation, you will receive clear instructions by email on how to safely provide your materials.

Available libraries

  • Family Library (FAM) — up to 1,000 entries
  • Community / Clan Library (COMM) — up to 2,000 entries
  • Photos / Docs / Memorabilia Add-On — optional, up to 1,000 attachments

You may also indicate if you want your library prepared in a print-ready layout (A4, A3, or book-style).

What happens after you submit

  1. Manual review — we check your request details and QR reference.
  2. Payment validation — proof is verified.
  3. Project creation — your Project ID is issued.
  4. Email guidance — you receive next steps, timelines, and instructions.

Submit your request

Tip: You may submit even if you are still gathering details — guidance will be provided after validation.

Handled with care

Each TinyTeam library is assembled with intention and delivered as a finished archive. Your data is handled privately, reviewed manually, and prepared for long-term preservation.

Optional print-ready formatting allows your library to exist as a physical keepsake if you choose.
