How the Process Works

  1. Choose Your Library & Style
    Select the type of library you need and the design style you prefer. This design will be applied exclusively to your project.
  2. Submit Your Materials
    You’ll be guided to submit names, relationships, photos, and documents. Our team carefully organizes and reviews everything.
  3. We Assemble & Deliver
    Your completed digital library is assembled, reviewed, and delivered as a private archive you can keep, share, and preserve.

About TinyTeam

TinyTeam Digital Library is a calm, human-handled system for preserving families, communities, and organizations through carefully assembled digital archives.

We focus on clarity, longevity, and respect for the stories entrusted to us. Every library is reviewed, assembled, and delivered with intention — not automated, not mass-produced.

TinyTeam is building a growing library of digital systems, beginning with genealogy, community records, and organizational hierarchies.
