How the Process Works

  1. Choose Your Library & Style
    Select the type of library you need and the design style you prefer. This design will be applied exclusively to your project.
  2. Submit Your Materials
    You’ll be guided to submit names, relationships, photos, and documents. Our team carefully organizes and reviews everything.
  3. We Assemble & Deliver
    Your completed digital library is assembled, reviewed, and delivered as a private archive you can keep, share, and preserve.

Designed to Grow with You

Each library is designed to comfortably support up to 1,000 individuals. Larger families, extended branches, or additional generations can continue their archive by adding a connected library.

Add Photos, Documents & Stories

We can carefully integrate photographs, letters, certificates, and written stories into your library — placing them alongside the people and timelines they belong to.

Additional collections can be added as your archive grows.

Built for Long-Term Preservation

Every library is delivered as a complete, client-owned archive. You receive your files in formats designed for long-term keeping, with the freedom to store, share, and preserve them independently.

Optional hosted access may be provided while the service remains active.

Introducing TinyTeam to Others

TinyTeam works with selected partners who help families and communities discover digital preservation. All libraries are still personally assembled and delivered by TinyTeam.
