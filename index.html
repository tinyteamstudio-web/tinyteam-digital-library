Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers — so you feel safe and confident before starting.

Is this public? Will my family information appear on Google?
No. TinyTeam is privacy-first. Your library is not published as a public directory and is not indexed by search engines. Access is shared only with people you choose.
How will my family easily access the library?
Your library is organized in a simple, clear structure that does not require special apps or technical skills. It is designed so even future generations can open, understand, and use it easily.
Will future generations still be able to access this?
Yes. The system is designed for long-term use. Your children, grandchildren, and future relatives can still access and understand the library years from now. This is built as a lasting reference — not a temporary tool.
What if some family members have never met each other?
That is very common. The library helps connect names, relationships, and branches even if people have never met personally. This helps clarify lineage and strengthen family identity over time.
Can this help connect our clan with other family branches?
Yes. When records are structured properly, it becomes easier to identify relationships between branches. Over time, families may discover shared lineage and reconnect through verified information.
What information do I need to start?
You can start with what you already have — names, relationships, key dates, and any known stories. Missing details are okay. We guide you during validation and help complete the structure.
What if some information is missing or uncertain?
That’s completely fine. You may submit partial information. During review, we validate what’s provided and request only what is needed to keep the library clean and accurate.
Can I update or add new relatives later?
Yes. Libraries can be updated over time. Families grow, records improve, and new connections may be discovered. Updates are handled carefully to preserve accuracy and continuity.
Do you offer print-ready or physical copies?
Yes. We can prepare print-ready formats so you can keep a physical copy, such as a family book or community archive, alongside the digital version.
How do payments work?
Payments are not auto-charged. Everything is manually verified. After submission, we confirm details and guide you through the next steps clearly and transparently.
