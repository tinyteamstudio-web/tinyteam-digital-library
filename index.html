Why Most Family & Community Records Get Lost

Histories disappear quietly — not because people don’t care, but because the process becomes hard to maintain.

Scattered information

Names, photos, and stories are spread across phones, chats, and folders — with no single place to return to.

No clear structure

Relationships and timelines get confusing fast without a clean format that makes sense to everyone.

Files get lost

Phones get replaced, accounts get forgotten, and storage gets messy — years of history can vanish overnight.

Privacy worries

Many families avoid online tools because they don’t want their information public or exposed.

No one continues it

One person starts collecting, but without a simple shared library, the work stops when life gets busy.

Not print-ready

People want something they can hold, but most systems don’t prepare a clean version for printing.

About TinyTeam

TinyTeam Digital Library is a human-handled, privacy-first system for organizing people, relationships, stories, and documents into a clean library that can be shared and preserved.

Our approach

  • Human-handled: we review and organize carefully, not auto-generated chaos.
  • Privacy-first: your data is not turned into a public profile or searchable database.
  • Simple + lasting: built to remain understandable even for future generations.
  • Print-ready: we can prepare formats suitable for a hard copy.

What happens after you submit

  1. You submit your request through the form.
  2. We review and validate details (manual).
  3. We confirm next steps and request any missing info.
  4. Your library is organized and prepared for long-term access.

You stay in control — nothing is auto-posted or made public.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers — so you feel safe and confident before starting.

Is this public? Will my family information appear on Google?
No. TinyTeam is designed as a privacy-first, human-handled library. We do not publish your information as a public directory.
Do you auto-charge payments?
No. Payments are not auto-processed. Everything is manually verified, by design.
Can I request a print-ready version?
Yes. We can prepare print-ready formats so you can keep a hard copy for your home or community archive.
What if I have missing details?
That’s okay. Submit what you have — we’ll guide you during validation and request only what’s needed.
How do add-ons work (Photos/Docs/Memorabilia)?
The add-on is used for organizing attachments such as photos, scanned documents, certificates, and memorabilia into your library.
Where can I ask questions or request updates?
Use the Help page or contact us via email — details are in the footer.
