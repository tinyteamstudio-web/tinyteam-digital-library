Thank you — we received your request

Your library request has been submitted successfully. TinyTeam is a human-handled process, and every submission is carefully reviewed.

If you do not receive an email reply within a reasonable time, please check your spam/junk folder, then reach out via the contact details below.

What happens next

  1. Review: We confirm the details of your request and check completeness.
  2. Follow-up: We’ll email you if anything is missing or needs clarification.
  3. Payment step: If applicable, we will confirm the amount and send payment instructions.
  4. Assembly: Your library is carefully assembled and prepared for delivery.
  5. Delivery: You receive your completed, client-owned archive.
