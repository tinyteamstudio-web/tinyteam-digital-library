Help & Guide

This guide helps you prepare your materials so TinyTeam can assemble your library smoothly and accurately. TinyTeam is a calm, human-handled, done-for-you process.

Every library is designed to become an heirloom, with optional print-ready formatting if you prefer a physical hard copy.

What to prepare

  • Library type: Family (FAM) or Community / Clan (COMM)
  • Design style: Heritage Classic, Modern Lineage, or Minimal Archive
  • People list: Names + optional notes (nickname, birth/death year, location)
  • Relationships: Parent/child, spouse/partner, siblings, key connections
  • Photos & documents (optional add-on): photos, certificates, letters, scanned pages
  • Stories & notes (optional): short stories, timelines, key memories

Library size & coverage

Family Library (FAM) supports up to 1,000 individuals.
Community / Clan Library (COMM) supports up to 2,000 individuals.

Privacy & handling

TinyTeam libraries are assembled through a careful, human-handled process. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive information you do not want included in the archive.

Your completed library is delivered as a client-owned archive designed for long-term keeping and independent storage.

What you will receive

  • Finished archive: a completed digital library output
  • Structured records: people, relationships, and linked materials
  • Print-ready option: layout prepared for A4, A3, or book-style hard copy (if requested)
  • Simple guidance: how to view, store, and preserve your archive
